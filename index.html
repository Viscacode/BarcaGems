Pedri

born Pedro González López on November 25, 2002, in Tegueste, Tenerife, Canary Islands, is a prodigious Spanish football talent who has swiftly risen as a centerpiece in both FC Barcelona's midfield and the Spanish national team. Known for his exceptional vision, precise passing, and intelligent movement, Pedri's style of play draws comparisons to Barcelona legends like Andrés Iniesta and Xavi Hernández. Despite his youth, Pedri exhibits a maturity on the pitch that belies his age, showcasing an innate understanding of the game's rhythms.

Gavi

born on August 5, 2004, in Los Palacios y Villafranca, Spain, represents a thrilling evolution in the lineage of midfield maestros at FC Barcelona. Bursting onto the scene with a blend of tenacity, technical skill, and an almost preternatural spatial awareness, Gavi quickly cemented his position as a key player in both Barcelona's rejuvenated squad and the Spanish national team. Known for his aggressive pressing, dribbling ability, and fearless approach to taking on opponents. storied midfield legacy.
